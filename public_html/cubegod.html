About this game

   In this city builder, you control the cube god, a legendary being with a simple design. Its flips and destroys all that get in its path. Creation has a path as well, build cities and towns, fortifications and defenses. The bug army is coming to take our your city as they don't worship such a god. Squish the invaders but careful not to destroy your own city in the process.
